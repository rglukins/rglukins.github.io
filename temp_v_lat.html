Temperature Near the Equator


Findings--

--Of the 4 factors I considered, temperature has the strongest causal relationship to latitude.

--On the date of analysis, the highest temperatures were recorded at 20 degrees latitude, which is due to the Earth's rotational position at that time of year. Summer in the Northern Hemisphere.

--We can say with confidence that the data supports the generally assumed correlation between latitute and temperature.
